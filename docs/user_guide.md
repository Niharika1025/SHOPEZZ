# ShopEZ Buyer User Guide

This guide helps buyers navigate the ShopEZ marketplace, browse items, write reviews, manage their carts, and complete payments.

---

## 1. Navigating the Catalog

### A. Browse and Search
- **Product Feed**: The catalog renders all approved product listings in a responsive grid.
- **Search**: Use the search bar in the filter panel to look for products by name.
- **Categories**: Select category options to view specific listings.

### B. Product Filtering & Sorting
- **Price Bounds**: Enter minimum and maximum pricing limits and click **Apply Price**.
- **Minimum Rating**: Filter by star rating threshold (4+, 3+, or 2+ stars).
- **Sort By**: Sort listings by newest first, price low-to-high, price high-to-low, or top-rated.

---

## 2. Product Details & Reviews

- **Product Details**: Click **View Details** on any product card to see description text, seller names, stock levels, and reviews.
- **Quantity Adjuster**: Adjust quantity inputs, bounded by available inventory.
- **Star Reviews**: Verified buyers (users who completed paid orders for the product) can leave a rating (1-5 stars) and feedback.

---

## 3. Cart & Payment Checkout

- **Shopping Cart**: Add products to your cart. Review item counts and subtotals on the Cart page (`/cart`).
- **Free Shipping**: Orders over $100 receive free shipping. Otherwise, a flat $10 shipping fee is added.
- **Secure Stripe Checkout**: From the Checkout page, fill in your shipping address and click **Place Order**. You will be redirected to Stripe's secure payment gateway.
- **Order Confirmation**: After a successful card payment, you will receive an automated email receipt, and the order will appear under **My Orders** with a status tracker.
